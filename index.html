INTRODUCTION

ZenHab Fashify is one of the leading manufacturers of leather apparels in Pakistan.

We produce leather garment from raw skin from own tannery to final product to fashion brands and retailers In Europe . such as Jackets , Dresses , shirts , skirts and trousers..

Sincere International is a Govt of Pakistan recognised export company. Our customer include some of the leading fashion brands and retail chain stores in Scandinavia / Europe and Canada. We work in wide varieties of skin which include Lamb, Goat, Cow, and Buffalo.
